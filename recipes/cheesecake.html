Cheesecake Recipe

Description

Cheesecake is a decadent dessert made with a rich, creamy filling consisting of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, all baked on top of a buttery graham cracker crust. It can be served plain or topped with various fruits, sauces, or chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Step 1: Prepare the crust

    Mix together graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.

  • Step 2: Prepare the filling

    In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Pour the filling over the crust.

  • Step 3: Bake

    Bake the cheesecake in a preheated oven at 325°F (160°C) for about 55-60 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.

  • Step 4: Chill

    Allow the cheesecake to cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

  • Step 5: Serve

    Remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and slice. Serve chilled, optionally topped with whipped cream, fruit compote, or chocolate sauce.
