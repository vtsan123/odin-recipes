Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and often other ingredients such as vegetables or herbs. The layers are typically baked until the pasta is cooked and the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a rich and hearty casserole-like dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce (ground beef or sausage and tomato sauce)
  • Cheese mixture (ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan)
  • Vegetables or herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare the ingredients

    Gather lasagna noodles, meat sauce (usually made with ground beef or sausage and tomato sauce), cheese mixture (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and any additional desired ingredients like vegetables or herbs.

  2. Step 2: Layer the ingredients

    In a baking dish, start by spreading a layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Then, add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of cheese mixture and any additional ingredients. Repeat these layers until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.

  3. Step 3: Bake

    Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven according to the recipe instructions, typically around 375°F (190°C) for about 45-60 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  4. Step 4: Serve

    Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh herbs or grated Parmesan cheese if desired, and enjoy!
